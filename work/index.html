Women Tech Careers

As part of the Harvard online course "CS50 Web Programming with Python and JavaScript" I have created a Job Portal dedicated to women in technology where candidates are able to create their own profiles and apply to job vacancies. Employers can also create a company profile and create job listings by paying a fee. Candidates can visit employer profiles and vice versa.
